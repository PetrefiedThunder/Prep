Sec. 117.3 - Definitions.
This section introduces key terms and context for Subpart B.
Additional detail line elaborating on how terms must be interpreted.

§ 117.4 Personnel qualifications and training.
Personnel must be qualified through education, training, or experience.
Training shall be documented and retained for review.

Sec. 117.8 – Equipment and utensils
Equipment and utensils must be designed to facilitate maintenance.
Sanitation schedules should reflect the complexity of the process.

Sec. 117.80(b)(1) -- Additional requirements for operations
Operations must maintain records demonstrating compliance.
Records shall be retained for at least two years.
